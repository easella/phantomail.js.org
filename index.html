Phantom Mail

What is this service for?

Phantom Mail is a free service that offers temporary, disposable mailboxes to receive emails from. No registration required! Simply click "new address" and then you can use your address to sign up for untrustworthy websites. Bear in mind, emails can be accessed by anyone that knows your address. Emails will be deleted every 10 days.

Can I use a custom address?

Yes. Simple click the "create inbox" button.
